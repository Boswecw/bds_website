Meet SMITH

SMITH is the workflow-intelligence layer used in Forge applications. It is designed to stay small, calm, and visible instead of turning the product into a noisy assistant theater.

What it is, and what it is not

SMITH exists to support structured work. It should clarify the next move, not compete with the operator for attention.

What it is

  • A workflow-aware guide inside Forge applications
  • A visible cue for state, guidance, and next-step clarity
  • An assistive layer designed around human control

What it is not

  • Not a generic chatbot occupying the whole screen
  • Not an autonomous worker deciding what happens next
  • Not an interruption engine that opens itself at random

Calm by design

SMITH belongs on the lower-right edge of the experience so it remains visible without pretending to be the main event.

Never auto-opens

Operator intent comes first. SMITH does not seize focus or interrupt the workflow uninvited.

Always under your control

It can be ignored, used when needed, or disabled in application settings if the operator wants a quieter surface.
